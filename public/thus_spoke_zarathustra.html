Thus Spoke Zarathustra: A Book for all and None is a work of philosophical fiction written by German philosopher Friedrick Nietzsche. It deals with ideas such as the Ubermensch, the death of God and the will to power.

1. Ubermensch

The Ubermensch (or Superman in (Nietzsche 1964)) is Nietzsche's idea for the next step of mankind. In (Nietzsche 1964), mankind is something to be overcome and a higher ideal should be strived for.

The opposite of the superman is the Ultimate Man.
